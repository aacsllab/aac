AUGMENTATIVE AND ALTERNATIVE COMMUNICATION

An Augmentative and Alternative Communication(AAC) device ,is a tablet or laptop that helps someone with a speech or language impairment to communicate. The term AAC devices is often used interchangeably with terms like Speech Generating Devices (SGD) for assistive communication device or simply communication device. AAC devices help users to communicate through a combination of words, sentenses and images that the device then “says out loud .” Additionally, users can also communicate by typing or drawing, sharing pictures or videos or even repeating words they hear out loud.
